Welcome to Asia Cup 2024

Experience the pinnacle of Asian cricket as six powerhouse nations compete for continental supremacy. The Asia Cup 2024 brings together the finest cricket teams from across the continent in a spectacular display of skill, strategy, and sporting spirit.

Two groups of three teams each, followed by Super 4s and the grand finale. Every match counts in this intense competition!

Watch the continent's best cricketers showcase their talents, including both seasoned veterans and exciting young prospects.
